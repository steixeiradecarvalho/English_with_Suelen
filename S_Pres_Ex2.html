Simple Present

Complete os espaços com o verbo entre parêntesis.

Dica:
Na afirmativa, quando for He / She / It (1ª pessoa), colocar o “S” no verbo
I play → She . jogar/ tocar/ brincar
They eat → He . comer
We sing → He . cantar
I talk → She . falar
We buy → He . comprar
I watch → She . assistir
They go → He . ir
I like → She . gostar
