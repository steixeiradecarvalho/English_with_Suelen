Lembrete:
He/She/It - coloca "s" no verbo nas afirmativas.
Nas interrogativas e negativas, remove "s" e usa "Does".
She goes to Paris
Ela vai para Paris
Where does she go?
Where do she go?
I buy a video-game
Eu comprei um videogame
What do you buy?
What does you buy?
He works as a doctor
Ele trabalha como médico
What do he do?
What does he do?
We eat a Japanese food, today
Nós comemos comida japonesa hoje
Where do you eat, today?
What do you eat, Today?
She listen her favorite song
Ela ouve a música favorita dela
Who does she listen?
What does she listen?
He listens his father
Ele ouve seu pai
Who does he listens?
Who does he listen?
